## 2. What you are giving us

You (or your child, with your consent) will download copies of your own data from the platforms checked above, using each platform's official "download your data" tool, and hand them to MyChoice through the personal hand-off method we arrange with you. You are never asked to share passwords or account access.

## 3. What MyChoice MAY do with this data

- **Inspect the exports** — open and read the files to understand what the platforms provide.
- **Derive signals** — compute patterns such as time-of-day activity, content volume, feed variety, and similar measures.
- **Create summaries** — produce readable summaries and visualizations of those patterns, shared with your family.
- **Use anonymized findings to improve product design** — keep general lessons (for example, "platform X's export lacks watch history") that contain no names, handles, message content, or other personal details.

## 4. What MyChoice may NOT do

- **We will not sell your data.** Not in alpha, not ever, not in anonymized form, not in any form.
- **We will not publish personal data** — no posts, messages, usernames, or identifiable patterns in any public material.
- **We will not share raw exports with unrelated third parties.** Raw files stay inside the approved alpha team.
- **We will not use raw exports to train unrelated external AI systems.** Your files are not training fodder.

## 5. Security — what is honestly true right now

We want your consent to be informed, so here is our actual security posture, without varnish:

- MyChoice is **alpha software**. Our systems are **not production-grade**: there is no certified security audit, no dedicated security operations team, and no compliance certification yet.
- Your data is processed **locally or in a controlled development environment** — small, closed, access-restricted machines, not a public cloud service open to the internet.
- Access is limited to **approved MyChoice alpha team members** who are bound by confidentiality obligations.
- Because no system is perfectly secure — and an alpha system especially — please only share data you are comfortable sharing under these conditions. You may exclude any platform, any account, or any file.

## 6. Deletion

- **You may request deletion at any time**, for any reason, without affecting your other agreements with MyChoice. We will confirm deletion to you when complete.
- **By default, raw export files are deleted after processing** is complete, unless you agree in writing that we may retain them longer (for example, to re-test improved parsers).
- **Derived anonymized learnings may be retained** after deletion of raw data. These contain no personal information — they are general findings about platforms and product design.

## 7. Children and teens

- Where the participant is a minor, **a parent or guardian must sign this document**, and we treat the minor's data with extra caution.
- **The child should be told, in age-appropriate language, what is happening** — that copies of their app activity are being shared with a small team to help build a tool for families. We can help you have that conversation, and we encourage the child's questions.
- A minor's participation can be ended by the parent/guardian — or by the minor telling us they want to stop — at any time, and deletion rules in Section 6 apply.
- We do not make compliance claims here (e.g., COPPA certification); rather, the alpha is run small and carefully precisely because those frameworks demand more than alpha systems can promise. Counsel review is required before any broader minor participation.

## 8. Confidentiality — both directions

- **MyChoice will treat your family's data as confidential**, as described above.
- **We ask you to treat the alpha as confidential too:** please do not publicly share screenshots, findings, documents, or other early product materials without our written permission. The product is unfinished, and early materials are easy to misread out of context.
- Nothing here stops you from talking generally about participating in a family digital-wellness study, or from exercising any legal right you have.

## 9. Plain-English summary

You are lending us a copy of your family's platform data so we can learn whether MyChoice can turn it into something genuinely useful for families. We look at it carefully, in a small closed environment, derive insights for *your family* and anonymized lessons for *the product*, and then delete the raw files unless you tell us otherwise. We don't sell it, publish it, hand it to outsiders, or feed it to unrelated AI. You can pull out at any time.
